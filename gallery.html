TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Before booking, it is important that you read our terms and conditions.

LATE POLICY
Clients are expected to arrive on time and failure to do so will incur the following:
- 5/10mins: Please message me that you are running late.
- 20 mins late: £10
- Any later: appointment automatically canceled.

RESCHEDULING/CANCELLATION POLICY Clients can reschedule their appointments but must give at least 24 hours' notice. As the deposit is non-refundable, any cancellation you make will mean you lose the deposit you paid.

TEHAIRUK’S RIGHTS

Clients with flu-like symptoms may be sent home if the condition will affect others. Additional people are not allowed to the appointment. We may request to see your ID.
